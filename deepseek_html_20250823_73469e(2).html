Why Join Us?

By joining the Ateker Business Forum, you gain access to a vibrant community of businesses across multiple sectors. Our platform helps you:

  • Increase your business visibility
  • Connect with potential clients and partners
  • Stay informed about industry trends
  • Access exclusive business resources
  • Participate in networking events

Our platform makes it easy to connect with businesses and customers:

  3. Get discovered by potential customers

  5. Grow your business through our network

About Ateker Business Forum

Our annual business networking conference
Business leaders from the Ateker community

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for business connectivity and growth within the Ateker community, fostering economic development and prosperity for all members through innovation, collaboration, and sustainable practices.

Our Mission

To connect businesses, facilitate partnerships, and create opportunities for entrepreneurs to thrive in a competitive market environment through our comprehensive business directory, networking events, and support services tailored to the needs of the Ateker community.

Our Values

  • Community: We believe in the power of community and collaboration
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in all our dealings
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and approaches to business development
  • Inclusivity: We welcome businesses of all sizes and sectors
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in all our services and interactions

Our History

Founded in 2015, the Ateker Business Forum began as a small initiative to connect local businesses in the region. Over the years, we have grown into a comprehensive platform serving hundreds of businesses across multiple sectors and countries.

Today, we continue to expand our services and reach, always with the goal of supporting economic growth and development within the Ateker community. Our platform now includes businesses from Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, South Sudan, and Ethiopia, creating a truly regional network of commerce and opportunity.
